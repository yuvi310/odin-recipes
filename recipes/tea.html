Prepare your Tea

Description:

One of the best Tea available in the whole world! Make your test better.

Ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamon
  • Tea Leaves
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Take Milk and water
  2. Boil them
  3. Mix sugar and cardamon depending upon your choices
  4. Let it boil
  5. Serve it
